📋 Import Results from Text

📊 Google Sheets: Select your result rows in Sheets, copy (Ctrl+C / ⌘C), then paste directly below. The columns are auto-detected — Season, Competition, Stage, Opponent, Venue (Home/Away/Neutral), Result (Won/Draw/Lost), Score and Goalscorers will all map automatically.
✏️ Manual text: One match per line. Pipe-separated: 2024/25 | Premier League | League | Arsenal | Home | Won | 2-1 | Saka (12)
Or freeform: Arsenal 2-1 Home Won  ·  Won 3-0 vs Liverpool Away
Set defaults above for Season/Competition/Stage to fill in any missing columns.
